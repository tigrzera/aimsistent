How to Play

  • Choose Mode: Dynamic (moving) or Static (still)
  • Time Limit: Standard (60s) or Quick Play (30s)
  • Click targets to score points
  • Optional: Use Z / X keys to shoot
  • Build your Combo to increase score
  • 5 hits increases score multiplier
  • Hit as many targets before the Timer Runs Out!
